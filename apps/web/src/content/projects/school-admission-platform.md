This freelance platform replaced a manual school admission process with one structured digital flow. Candidates can complete large multi-step forms with autosave, then upload all required documents.

Admins get status management, filtered review views, and Excel export endpoints for operations. On the backend, upload and file-key validation keeps submissions consistent before they enter review.

It is a practical project shaped by messy real-world form behavior, not by ideal demo flows.
