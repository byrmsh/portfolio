## Why this exists

Song lyrics are high-context language input. I wanted a faster way to turn saved tracks into reusable study material.

## What it currently does

- Ingests saved tracks.
- Extracts key vocabulary and contexts.
- Produces flashcard-ready note structures.

## Planned upgrades

- Add sentence-level difficulty scoring.
- Add spaced-repetition analytics.
